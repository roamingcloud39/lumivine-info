Child Safety Standards

Lumivine's Commitment to Protecting Children
Effective Date: August 15, 2025
Last Updated: August 15, 2025
At Lumivine, the safety of children is our highest priority. We maintain zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content, and we are committed to creating a safe environment for all users through proactive prevention, detection, and response measures.

Our Zero Tolerance Commitment

Lumivine has zero tolerance for any content, behavior, or activity that sexually exploits or endangers children. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or solicitation of minors
  • Sexualization of minors
  • Trafficking or exploitation of children

Age Verification and Access Control

  • Minimum Age Requirement: Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account on Lumivine
  • Age Verification: We implement age verification mechanisms during account creation
  • Account Termination: Accounts identified as belonging to users under 13 are immediately terminated
  • Parental Controls: We provide resources for parents to understand our platform's safety features

Prevention Measures

1. Technology-Based Prevention:
  • Automated content scanning using industry-standard detection technologies
  • PhotoDNA and similar hash-matching technologies to identify known CSAM
  • Machine learning models to detect potentially harmful content and behaviors
  • Keyword filtering and behavioral pattern analysis
2. Design-Based Safety:
  • Private messaging restrictions for minors
  • Enhanced privacy settings for younger users
  • Limitations on adult-minor interactions
  • Restricted discoverability of minor accounts
3. Community Guidelines:
  • Clear prohibitions against CSAE in our Terms of Service
  • Regular user education about child safety
  • Prominent reporting mechanisms throughout the app

Detection and Response

1. Content Moderation:
  • 24/7 human moderation team trained in child safety protocols
  • Priority queue for child safety reports
  • Immediate action on verified CSAE content
  • Preservation of evidence for law enforcement
2. User Reporting:
  • Easy-to-access reporting buttons on all content and profiles
  • Dedicated child safety reporting category
  • Anonymous reporting options
  • Confirmation of report receipt and action taken
3. Investigation Process:
  • Immediate content removal upon verification
  • Account suspension during investigation
  • Permanent ban for confirmed violations
  • Device and IP blocking for repeat offenders

Law Enforcement Cooperation

Lumivine cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies and child safety organizations:
  • NCMEC Reporting: We report all instances of apparent child sexual abuse to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • CyberTipline: Active participation in NCMEC's CyberTipline program
  • Evidence Preservation: Retention of relevant data for law enforcement investigations
  • Global Cooperation: Collaboration with international child safety organizations and law enforcement
  • Legal Compliance: Full compliance with all applicable laws including FOSTA-SESTA, COPPA, and regional regulations

⚠️ Immediate Danger

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, please contact:

  • Emergency Services: 911 (US) or your local emergency number
  • NCMEC CyberTipline: www.cybertipline.org
  • FBI: tips.fbi.gov
  • Local Law Enforcement: Contact your local police department

Education and Resources

We provide comprehensive safety resources:
  • In-app safety center with child protection information
  • Parent/guardian guides for online safety
  • Age-appropriate safety tips for young users
  • Regular safety awareness campaigns
  • Partnership with child safety organizations for educational content

Transparency and Accountability

  • Regular Audits: Third-party audits of our child safety practices
  • Transparency Reports: Annual publication of child safety statistics and actions taken
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular review and enhancement of safety measures
  • Industry Collaboration: Active participation in technology coalitions for child safety

Training and Staff Support

  • Comprehensive training for all staff on child safety protocols
  • Specialized training for moderation teams
  • Mental health support for staff reviewing sensitive content
  • Regular updates on emerging threats and prevention techniques
