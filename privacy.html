Privacy Policy

Lumivine App
Effective Date: July 9, 2025
Last Updated: July 9, 2025
Lumivine is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and services.

What information do we collect?

Information you give us includes your name, email, profile details, posts, comments, messages, photos, videos, and other content you share. We also collect information about your participation in community features.
We automatically collect basic usage information to help improve our app, along with your device type and operating system for compatibility. We also collect basic technical information needed for the app to work properly and stay secure.
If you connect social media accounts, we may receive information according to those platforms' terms. We use analytics services to understand how the app is used so we can make it better.

How do we use your information?

We use your information to provide and improve our service, manage your account, enable communication within our community, personalize your experience, send you important notifications, provide customer support, keep the platform safe, and comply with legal requirements.

Who do we share information with?

We share your profile and content with other users according to your privacy settings. We work with trusted service providers who help us operate the app. We may share information when required by law or to protect safety. In business transfers like mergers, your information may be transferred. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

How do we protect your information?

We use appropriate security measures to protect your information, though no internet transmission is completely secure.

Your choices and rights

You can update your profile and privacy settings, control who sees your content, and manage notifications. You can request information about your data, correct your information through profile settings, contact us to delete your account and data, or request a copy of your data.

Children's privacy

Our service is not for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13.

International transfers

Your information may be processed in other countries with appropriate safeguards in place.

Policy changes

We may update this policy and will notify you of material changes through the app or email. Continued use means you accept the updates.

Contact us

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at lumivine@lumivine.app
This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations.
